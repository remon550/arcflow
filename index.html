How it Works

Three steps to instant USDC payments

No gas tokens to buy, no ETH to bridge. Just connect, type an address, and send.

01
🦊
Connect MetaMask

One click to connect your MetaMask wallet. ArcFlow automatically adds Arc Testnet to your network list and switches you over.

02
💵
Enter Amount & Address

Type or paste a wallet address and enter the USDC amount you want to send. Your balance updates in real time from the Arc RPC.

03
⚡
Confirm & Track

Approve the transaction in MetaMask. ArcFlow polls the blockchain and shows you a live status page until your USDC arrives.

Why ArcFlow

Everything USDC payments need

Built ground-up for Arc Network's unique architecture where USDC is the native currency.

⚡
Sub-second Finality

Arc Network's fast consensus means your USDC transfers confirm in under a second. No more watching a spinner for minutes.

💚
USDC as Gas

Pay gas fees directly in USDC — no need to hold ETH or any other token. One token for everything on Arc.

🔒
Non-Custodial

ArcFlow never holds your keys or your funds. Everything is signed locally in MetaMask and broadcast directly to Arc RPC.

📱
Mobile-First UI

Fully responsive interface designed for sending on the go. Looks great on any screen from a 4-inch phone to a 4K monitor.

🔗
Live Chain Explorer

Every transaction links to ArcScan so you can verify your payments on-chain, share receipts, or audit your history.

🧠
Smart Contact Memory

Recent recipients are saved locally so you can resend to frequent contacts with one tap — no copy-pasting addresses again.
